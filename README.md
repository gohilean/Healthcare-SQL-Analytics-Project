# Healthcare-SQL-Analytics-Project
Healthcare analytics project using PostgreSQL and SQL to analyze 10,000 patient records and generate business insights.

Key Findings
Query 1: Total Revenue by Insurance Provider

Finding: Cigna generated the highest total billing revenue at approximately $52.34 million, followed closely by Aetna and Blue Cross.

Query 2: Average Cost by Medical Condition

Finding: Diabetes had the highest average billing cost at approximately $26,060 per patient, while Arthritis had the lowest at approximately $25,188.

Query 3: Average Length of Stay

Finding: Patients stayed in the hospital an average of 15.56 days.

Query 4: Average Billing by Admission Type

Finding: Urgent admissions generated the highest average billing amount (~$25,961), followed by Elective and Emergency admissions.

Query 5: Patient Count by Medical Condition

Finding: Asthma was the most common condition (1,708 patients), followed closely by Cancer (1,703 patients).

Query 6: Patient Count by Blood Type

Finding: AB- was the most common blood type in the dataset with 1,275 patients.

Query 7: Patient Count by Insurance Provider

Finding: Cigna served the largest patient population with 2,040 patients.

Query 8: Total Revenue by Insurance Provider

Finding: Cigna generated the highest total revenue at approximately $52.34 million.

Query 9: Total Revenue by Admission Type

Finding: Urgent admissions generated the highest total revenue at approximately $88.03 million.

Query 10: Total Revenue by Medical Condition

Finding: Cancer generated the highest total revenue at approximately $43.49 million.

Query 11: Average Length of Stay by Medical Condition

Finding: Arthritis patients had the longest average hospital stay at 15.99 days.

Query 12: Top 10 Doctors by Patient Volume

Finding: Michael Johnson handled the highest number of patients (7), while several physicians treated 5 patients each.

Query 13: Revenue Per Day by Medical Condition

Finding: Diabetes generated the highest revenue per hospitalization day at approximately $1,673/day.

Query 14: Average Billing by Gender

Finding: Male and female patients had nearly identical average healthcare costs (~$25.5K per patient), indicating no significant cost difference by gender.

Query 15: Patient Count by Admission Type

Finding: Urgent admissions were the most common (3,391 patients), though all admission categories were relatively balanced.
